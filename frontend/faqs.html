Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: How do I create an account?

Click on the "Login" button in the top right corner and select "Register here". Fill in your details and you're ready to shop!

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and Cash on Delivery (COD) for eligible orders.

Q: How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-7 business days. Express shipping is available for faster delivery.

Returns & Refunds

Q: What is your return policy?

We offer a hassle-free 10-day return policy. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

Q: How do I initiate a return?

Go to "My Orders", select the item you want to return, and click "Return Item". Our team will guide you through the process.

Q: When will I get my refund?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your returned item.
